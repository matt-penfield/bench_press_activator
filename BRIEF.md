# Leader Dashboard — Brief

## Overview
A dashboard for leaders at a digital agency to view their team members grouped by survey-assigned persona.

## Personas
Team members are assigned one of five personas after completing a survey:
- **Confidant**
- **Activator**
- **Realist**
- **Debater**
- **Expert**

*(Persona details TBD)*

## Leader Profile
The dashboard displays the leader's:
- **Role** (their position/title)
- **Market** (the market they operate in within the agency)

## Core Feature
- View team members grouped by their assigned persona
